# Getprospect

Find and verify B2B email addresses, and manage leads, companies, and outreach sequences. Search for prospect email addresses by name and domain, verify email deliverability, and organize contacts with tags and notes. Create and manage email outreach sequences with configurable steps. Receive webhook notifications when new contacts are saved or emails are found.
