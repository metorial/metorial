# <img src="logo.svg" height="20"> Logo.dev

Retrieve company logos, brand data, and enrichment information from a database of tens of millions of companies. Look up logo images by domain, stock ticker, cryptocurrency symbol, ISIN, or company name with configurable format, size, theme, and greyscale options. Search for company domains by brand name with autocomplete support. Retrieve comprehensive brand enrichment data including company descriptions, brand colors, blurhash placeholders, and social media links across platforms like LinkedIn, Twitter/X, GitHub, and more.
