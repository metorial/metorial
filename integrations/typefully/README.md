# <img src="logo.png" height="20"> Typefully

Create, schedule, and publish social media content across X (Twitter), LinkedIn, Threads, Bluesky, and Mastodon. Manage multi-platform drafts with per-platform tailored content, schedule posts for specific times or queue them into available slots, and publish immediately. Upload and attach media (images, videos, GIFs, PDFs) to posts. Organize content with tags, manage social sets (groups of connected platform accounts), and filter drafts by status. Receive webhook notifications for draft lifecycle events including creation, scheduling, publishing, status changes, tag changes, and deletion.
