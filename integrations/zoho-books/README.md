# <img src="logo.svg" height="20"> Zoho Books

Manage small and medium business accounting including invoicing, expense tracking, payments, and financial reporting. Create and send invoices, estimates, sales orders, purchase orders, and bills with full lifecycle management. Record customer and vendor payments, issue credit notes and vendor credits, and process refunds. Track expenses with receipt attachments and manage recurring expenses. Manage contacts (customers and vendors) with billing/shipping addresses and payment terms. Handle banking operations including bank statement imports, transaction categorization, and matching. Manage chart of accounts, journal entries, fixed assets, inventory items, taxes, and multi-currency exchange rates. Create and track projects with tasks, time entries, and billing. Configure organization settings, users, roles, and reporting tags. Attach documents to transactions and send emails with customizable templates. Receive webhook notifications for events on invoices, contacts, expenses, bills, payments, and other modules.
