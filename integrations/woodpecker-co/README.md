# <img src="logo.png" height="20"> Woodpecker Co

Create, manage, and automate B2B cold email and LinkedIn outreach campaigns. Build multi-step sequences with configurable delays, A/B testing, and delivery time windows. Add, update, search, and manage prospects with custom fields, statuses, and interest levels. Track engagement including opens, clicks, and replies. Manage connected email mailboxes and LinkedIn accounts. Maintain domain and email blacklists. Generate asynchronous campaign performance reports. Handle manual tasks for prospects. Subscribe to webhooks for prospect status changes, engagement events, campaign events, LinkedIn events, and task events. Supports agency-level management of multiple client accounts.
