# <img src="logo.png" height="20"> Benzinga

Access financial news, market data, company fundamentals, and alternative datasets. Retrieve real-time headlines, full articles, and press releases filtered by ticker, channel, or date. Query financial calendars for earnings, dividends, IPOs, analyst ratings, FDA events, mergers & acquisitions, and stock splits. Get company profiles, financial statements, valuation ratios, and key statistics. Identify top market movers (gainers, losers, most active). Access unusual options activity signals, delayed quotes, historical OHLCV price bars, short interest data, SEC filings, and government/insider trade records. Retrieve corporate logos with light/dark variants and brand colors. Receive real-time news via webhooks or WebSocket streaming. Supports delta queries to efficiently fetch only recently updated records.
