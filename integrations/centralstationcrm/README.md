# <img src="logo.png" height="20"> Central Station Crm

Manage contacts, deals, and sales pipelines in CentralStationCRM. Create, read, update, and delete people and company records with addresses, contact details, and avatars. Track deals with volume, probability, and due dates. Manage tasks assigned to users and linked to contacts, deals, or projects. Store and retrieve notes, emails, and communication history on contact records. Tag and filter people, companies, and deals by custom keywords. Convert won deals into projects with task lists. Record milestones and life events for personalized outreach. Retrieve CRM statistics including deal counts and value sums. Configure webhooks to monitor creation, update, and deletion of CRM objects.
