# Hunter

Find, verify, and enrich professional email addresses and company data. Search for all emails associated with a domain, find specific contacts by name or LinkedIn handle, and verify email deliverability. Enrich person and company profiles with detailed information including employment details, social profiles, technographics, industry classifications, and funding data. Discover companies using natural language queries or structured filters. Manage leads and lead lists, run email outreach sequences, and track sequence events like opens, clicks, and replies via webhooks. Retrieve company logos and check account credit usage.
