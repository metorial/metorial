# <img src="logo.png" height="20"> Safety Culture

Manage workplace inspections, safety issues, corrective actions, assets, and team training on the SafetyCulture (iAuditor) platform. Create, start, complete, and export inspections using configurable templates. Track and assign corrective actions with priorities, due dates, and statuses. Create and manage issues (incidents) with categories, assignees, and timelines. Organize assets with custom types and fields. Administer users, groups, permissions, and site hierarchies. Schedule recurring inspections. Access training courses, lessons, and analytics. Manage documents, credentials, contractor companies, OSHA recordkeeping, IoT sensors, and investigations. Export data incrementally via data feeds for syncing to external warehouses. Receive webhook notifications for inspection, action, issue, media, and training events.
