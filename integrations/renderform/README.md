# <img src="logo.svg" height="20"> Renderform

Generate images and PDFs from templates with dynamic text, images, QR codes, and custom properties. Manage and list templates with placeholder definitions. Take website screenshots with configurable dimensions and wait times. Browse previously rendered results and their CDN-hosted URLs. Receive webhook notifications for render completion and template lifecycle events.
