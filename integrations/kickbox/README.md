# <img src="logo.svg" height="20"> Kickbox

Verify email addresses for deliverability, detect disposable, role-based, and free email domains, and identify typos in email addresses. Supports single real-time email verification and batch verification of up to 1 million addresses. Returns deliverability status (deliverable, undeliverable, risky, unknown), a Sendex quality score, and suggested corrections for misspelled domains. Useful for cleaning email lists, reducing bounce rates, and validating user-submitted emails.
