# <img src="logo.png" height="20"> Callingly

Automate outbound and inbound phone calls to connect sales teams with leads. Create and manage leads with contact details, status, tags, and assigned owners. Initiate and schedule calls, retrieve call details including recordings and transcripts, and send personalized SMS messages. Configure teams with call modes, whisper text, retry settings, and auto-SMS. Manage agent availability, scheduling, priorities, and team assignments. Set up webhooks for call completed, lead created, and lead updated events. Supports agency partners managing multiple client accounts.
