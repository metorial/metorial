# <img src="logo.png" height="20"> Yelp

Search for local businesses by keyword, category, location, phone number, price level, and transaction type. Retrieve detailed business information including name, address, phone, photos, ratings, hours, and price levels. Access review excerpts and review highlights for businesses. Provide real-time autocomplete suggestions for business, term, and category searches. Match businesses by name and address to obtain Yelp Business IDs. Use conversational AI chat for natural language local business discovery. Manage business listings, respond to reviews, handle leads, book reservations, and manage advertising programs via partner APIs. Retrieve engagement metrics, advertising performance data, and location analytics. Subscribe to webhooks for review and lead event notifications.
