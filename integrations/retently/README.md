# <img src="logo.png" height="20"> Retently

Manage customer experience surveys including NPS, CSAT, CES, and 5-Star metrics. Create, update, and delete customers with custom properties and tags. Send transactional surveys via email or in-app channels with optional delivery delays. Retrieve and annotate survey responses with topics, sentiments, and tags. Access campaign reports, score trends, and delivery statistics. Manage survey suppression lists and customer unsubscriptions. Monitor webhook events for new responses, score updates, bounces, and unsubscribes.
