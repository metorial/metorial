# <img src="logo.png" height="20"> Ambee

Retrieve hyperlocal environmental and climate data across 37 endpoints. Query real-time, historical, and forecast air quality (AQI, pollutant concentrations), weather conditions (temperature, humidity, wind, pressure), pollen counts (tree, grass, weed), wildfire activity and fire-risk forecasts, natural disaster data (earthquakes, floods, droughts, cyclones, volcanic eruptions), soil moisture and temperature, water vapor levels, NDVI/EVI vegetation indices, and elevation data. Data can be queried by latitude/longitude, postal code, city name, or country code. Access map tile layers for air quality and pollen visualization. Configure webhook-based environmental alerts for pollen, air quality, weather, and wildfires with custom conditions and frequency. Supports multi-language responses and cloud data dumps to S3, GCP, Azure, or FTP.
