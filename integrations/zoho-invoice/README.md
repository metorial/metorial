# <img src="logo.svg" height="20"> Zoho Invoice

Create, send, and manage invoices, estimates, credit notes, and recurring invoices for customers. Record customer payments, apply credits, and process refunds. Track expenses (billable and non-billable) and manage expense categories. Create and manage projects with tasks, user assignments, and time tracking with start/stop timers. Manage a catalog of items, price lists, tax rates, and multi-currency exchange rates. Handle customer and contact management with billing/shipping addresses and portal access. Send invoices and estimates via email individually or in bulk. Supports GST, VAT, and regional tax treatments across multiple Zoho data center regions.
