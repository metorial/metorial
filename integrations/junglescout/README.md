# <img src="logo.jpeg" height="20"> Jungle Scout

Access Amazon marketplace intelligence data across 10 supported marketplaces (US, UK, DE, IN, CA, FR, IT, ES, MX, JP). Research keywords by ASIN or seed keyword to retrieve search volume, competition metrics, and suggested bid data. Query historical weekly search volume for trend and seasonality analysis. Search Amazon's product catalog with filters on category, price, and attributes to find matching ASINs with sales estimates. Retrieve daily historical sales estimates and pricing for specific ASINs over a date range. Analyze brand Share of Voice on keywords for competitive positioning insights.
