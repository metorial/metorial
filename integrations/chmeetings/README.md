# <img src="logo.svg" height="20"> Chmeetings

Manage church members, families, groups, contributions, and organizational structures in ChMeetings. Retrieve and delete people records and profile notes. Create and manage family structures including adding, updating, and removing family members. Pull groups and ministries data within the church hierarchy. Record and retrieve contributions (donations), and track pledges and campaigns. Receive real-time webhook notifications when people are created, updated, or deleted, and when contributions are created.
