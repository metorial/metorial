# Linkedin Ads

Create, manage, and optimize LinkedIn advertising campaigns targeting professional audiences. Manage the full ad hierarchy including ad accounts, campaign groups, campaigns, and creatives. Configure audience targeting based on professional attributes like job function, industry, seniority, and company size. Pull campaign analytics and reporting data including impressions, clicks, engagement, conversions, and cost metrics. Track online and offline conversions via server-side events. Collect and sync leads from LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms to external systems. Upload and manage media assets (images, videos, documents) for ad creatives. Create and promote LinkedIn events through ad campaigns. Receive real-time webhook notifications for lead form submissions and organization social actions.
