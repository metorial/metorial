# <img src="logo.svg" height="20"> Ritekit

Analyze and suggest hashtags for social media posts based on real-time popularity and trending data. Detect banned Instagram hashtags. Generate styled quote images and animated GIFs from text or static images. Extract company logos, brand colors, and resolve company names to domains. Validate and enrich email addresses by detecting freemails, disposable emails, typos, and extracting names. Suggest and auto-insert emojis into text. Shorten links with optional call-to-action overlays. Extract article content, top images, and link previews from URLs.
