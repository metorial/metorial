# Nusii Proposals

Create, send, track, and manage business proposals for freelancers and agencies. Manage clients with contact details, preferred currency, and locale settings. Build proposals from reusable templates with text and cost sections, including line items with fixed, recurring, or per-unit pricing. Send proposals to multiple recipients with configurable subject, message, CC/BCC, and signer eligibility. Track proposal activity including client views, PDF downloads, email opens, acceptances, and rejections. Filter proposals by status (draft, pending, accepted, rejected, clarification). Manage sections and line items within proposals and templates. Retrieve account info, users, and available themes. Subscribe to webhooks for proposal and client lifecycle events.
