# <img src="logo.png" height="20"> Crustdata

Enrich, search, and monitor B2B company and people data in real time. Look up companies by domain, name, or ID to retrieve 250+ data points including headcount, funding, web traffic, job openings, and product reviews. Screen and filter companies by firmographic and growth criteria to build targeted prospect lists. Enrich professional profiles using LinkedIn URLs, emails, or name-company combinations to get employment history, skills, and contact info. Search for people by title, location, seniority, function, and recent job changes. Retrieve structured job listings with 30+ data points and 35 search filters. Access social media posts with engagement metrics for people and companies. Perform structured web searches returning entity-linked results for press releases, funding news, and product launches. Subscribe to real-time webhook notifications for job changes, funding rounds, headcount shifts, new job postings, and social media activity via the Watcher API.
