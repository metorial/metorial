# Lob

Send and track physical mail pieces including postcards, letters, self-mailers, checks, snap packs, and booklets programmatically. Verify and standardize US and international addresses across 240+ countries. Manage reusable address books and HTML templates with merge variables for dynamic personalization. Create direct mail campaigns with audience uploads and creative templates. Embed trackable QR codes and short links on mail pieces. Schedule mailings up to 180 days in advance, track delivery status via USPS events, and receive webhook notifications for mail lifecycle events. Validate identity associations between names and addresses. Send checks from verified bank accounts.
